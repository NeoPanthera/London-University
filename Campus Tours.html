Campus Tours

If you're keen to visit London University and can't wait for an open day, come along on one of our campus tours. After a short welcome by a member of our Student Recruitment team, you will be shown around our facilities by one of our current students. Afterwards you will have the chance to speak to a member of the Student Recruitment team if you have any queries about our courses and admission.

Tours take place on most months of the year and will take place on the following dates in 2013:

Wednesday 31 July. Booking for this date is now open. Book a place now.

The following dates will be available for booking soon.

Wednesday 21 August

Wednesday 25 September

Wednesday 30 October

Wednesday 27 November

Please note that we are unable to guarantee that your tour will be led by a student studying your course of interest.

Booking for each campus tour will open approximately two months before the date. Campus tours run in the afternoon and last approximately one hour.

Please note that it is likely that you will be able to bring one guest only on the tour. We may be able to accommodate more guests on the day but this is not guaranteed.
